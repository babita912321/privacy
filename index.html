glassWatch Privacy Policy

Welcome to glassWatch! This privacy policy outlines how we handle your data and your privacy rights when using our stopwatch mobile application.

Data Collection and Usage

glassWatch is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect any user data, including personal information. Our app does not request or require any special permissions that would compromise your privacy. Your stopwatch activities, such as timing sessions and button interactions, are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

glassWatch does not integrate with any third-party services or applications that would involve the sharing of your data. We do not sell, rent, or share any user information with third parties for marketing or other purposes.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please feel free to contact us at glasswatch@gma.io. We value your feedback and are dedicated to addressing any inquiries you may have regarding your privacy and the use of our app.

Security Measures

glassWatch employs industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. We continuously assess and enhance our security practices to ensure the confidentiality of your information.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our privacy policy as necessary to reflect changes in our app's features, legal requirements, or user feedback. Any modifications will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.

Conclusion

Thank you for choosing glassWatch. Your privacy is important to us, and we are dedicated to maintaining the security and confidentiality of your information. If you have any further questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.
